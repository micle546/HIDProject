Birds

Birds are animals that are born from hard-shelled eggs and have feathers as well as wings.

Wings, however, are not the defining feature of birds because there are other animals that have wings. Some of these animals include bats and certain insects, such as flies.

Birds are the only animals that have feathers on their body, so this is the feature that distinguishes them from all other animals. The feathers on a bird's body help them to steer their wings while flying. A bird's feathers also help to keep it warm in cold temperatures, and sometimes they are used to attract a significant other through mating rituals.
