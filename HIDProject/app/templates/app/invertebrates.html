Invertebrates

Invertebrates classify all animals that don't have a spinal cord. As you can imagine, this means that there are quite a few of them! In fact, invertebrates make up most life on Earth.

Some typical invertebrates that you may be thinking of is insects like bees and ants, arachnids like spiders, shellfish like lobsters and crabs, arthropods like centipedes and millipedes, and many more!

In addition to the typical animals that you may think of when thinking about invertebrates, sea sponges, urchins, and anemones are also invertebrates, and are therefore animals.

While generally being thought of as simple and unintelligent, some invertebrates are quite intelligent, such as octopi and other cephalopods.
